Dimitri Leslie Roger Wiki Biography

Dimitri Leslie Roger was born on 13 July 1992 in Queens, New York City, USA, into a family of Haitian ancestry, and under the stage name Rich The Kid is best known as a rapper, who has released mixtapes such as ‘’Been About the Benjamins’’ and ‘’Keep Flexin’’’.

So just how wealthy is Rich The Kid as of late 2017? According to authoritative sources, this rapper has a net worth of over $2 million, accumulated from his career in the previously mentioned field, including as a songwriter, record executive and producer, as well as an actor.

Rich The Kid Net Worth $2 Million

Rich The Kid moved to Woodstock, Georgia with his now divorced mother, and was reportedly interested in music from an early age, growing-up listening to the music of artists such as Jay-Z and 50 Cent. He began his career by releasing an EP entitled ‘’Been About the Benjamins’’ in 2013, consisting of 19 songs including ‘’On My Pradas’’ and ‘’Ready To Die’’, and in collaboration with plenty of artists such as Migos and D Dash. He followed by releasing a collaborative mixtape during the same year, made with Migos, one of the most popular hip hop groups as of today, lifting his net worth significantly.

Ultimately, the team made the series of mixtapes entitled ‘’Streets On Lock’’, released in three parts, with the latest one released in 2014. Subsequently, Rich The Kid released his second solo mixtape ‘’Feels Good 2 Be Rich’’, made in collaboration with notable names from the rap world, Yung Thug and Kirko Bangzz among others. The mixtape featured 19 tracks like ‘’Came from Nothin’’’ and ‘’Too Much’’. He remained busy, working on another project in the late 2014 and made the single ‘’On My Way’’ with Bobby Shmurda and Rowdy Rebel. Having established a successful partnership with Migos, he made another mixtape with them in 2015, entitled ‘’Still On Lock’’ and in the following year, had a solo project, recording ‘’Flexin’ on Purpose’’, teamed up with Fetty Wap and Young Dolph among others, and followed the 14 track mixtape by releasing ‘’Streets On Lock 4’’, which featured 27 songs made with Young Dolph, 2 Chainz and Peewee Longway. Subsequently, he worked with iLoveMakonnen, and the duo released eight tracks on ‘’Whip It’’, on Thanksgiving. On Christmas Eve of 2015, he released ‘’Dabbin’ Fever’’, in collaboration with Migos once again, but also with Wiz Khalifa and Kodak Black, and in the following year, he made another mixtape entitled ‘’Trap Talk’’. When it comes to his latest projects, in 2017 he teamed up with Diplo, Justin Bieber and Yung Thug on ‘’Bankroll’’, adding steadily to his net worth.

Besides being a singer himself, he is also the owner of the record label Rich Forever Music; the label is signed with artists like Famous Dex and is working closely with 300 Entertainment, which is now the parent company of Rich Forever Music.

When it comes to his private life, Rich The Kid doesn’t share much information regarding that topic, however, we know that he is married to dancer Lady Luscios, and the couple has a son, King Rich Roger. He is active on social media websites such as Instagram and Twitter and is followed by more than two million people on the former.
