Who is Eiza González?

Eiza González is a Mexican actress and singer. She has been critically acclaimed for her debut role as Dolores “Lola” Valente in the Mexican musical telenovela Lola…Érase Una Vez in the year 2007.

In 2014, she has starred in an American horror series From Dusk till Dawn: The Series.

Eiza González: Birth facts, Parents, and Childhood

Eiza was born on 30th January 1990, in Mexico City, Mexico. Her birth name is Eiza González Reyna. Moreover, her ethnicity is Mexican and her nationality is Mexican.

Eiza González’s mother’s name is Glenda Reyna and her father’s name is Carlos González. She has a brother, Yulen González Reyna and he is twelve years older than her. She does not have any sisters.

When she was twelve years old, her father expired due to a motorcycle accident.

Eiza González: Early Professional Life and Career

While learning acting at Patricia Reyes Spíndola’s M & M Studio, Eiza González caught the eye of producer and director, Pedro Damian and she was cast in Lola…Érase Una Vez.

In 2009, she has starred in a television series, Mujeres Asesinas and Sueña conmigo in 2011. In 2010, she released her solo album, Contracorriente. And other series in which she was featured in are Miss XV, Casi 30, Amores verdaderos, Amor en custodia, Estrellas in Mexico.

Eiza González has appeared in the series which gained so much fame especially teens, Gossip Girl: Acapulco, which is the Mexican remake of Gossip Girl. In 2017, she did an action movie Baby Driver.

Apart from acting, she has hosted MTV Millennial Awards. She was the face of Color Trend de Avon in 2008. Moreover, she is the brand ambassador for brands like Asepxia, Neutrogena, etc.
