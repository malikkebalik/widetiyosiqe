Amy Banse's Career and Achievements 

Amy Banse started her career as an in-house attorney at Comcast in 1991. Initially, she was responsible for overseeing the development of Comcast's cable network portfolio, including the company's investments in E! Entertainment Television, The Golf Channel, and VERSUS. Also, the launch of G4, PBS KIDS Sprout, TV One, and Comcast's sports networks happened under her guidance.

While at Comcast in 2000, Amy invested $5 million into the Women's United Soccer Association, the world's first women's professional soccer league. The league was suspended in 2003 due to low television ratings and low attendance.
