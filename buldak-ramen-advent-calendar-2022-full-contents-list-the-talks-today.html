More About Buldak Ramen’s Advent Calendar

With her initial video receiving 5.4 million views, food blogger OhMyMisty is the sole creative online displaying her ramen advent calendar.

The item is packaged in a sizable cardboard box that has been festively adorned for the holidays with its customary adorable cartoon characters. Samyang Foods, the company behind Buldak, is not minimalistic, in contrast to other businesses that have reduced their original calendars down to 12 days. There are 24 slots in the calendar, which translates to 24 Buldak Ramen variations.

OhMyMisty has so far conquered the first five days. So, let’s find out more about what she got on all 5 days:

  • Day 1: The original Artificial Spicey Chicken Flavor, which is where the popular Fire Noodle Challenge got its start.
  • Day 2: Pasta with chicken and tomatoes is something we had never seen in supermarkets. This is the choice for you if you want spicy pasta.
  • Day 3: The next dish was cream carbonara, which delivered a tremendous flavor punch and a welcome respite from the stifling heat. OhMyMisty gave it a flavor rating of nine out of ten and a spice rating of seven out of ten.
  • Day 4: The blogger started crying for her stomach even before she finished the 2x Spicy Chicken served on this particular day.
  • Day 5: This day featured their cheesy Chicken since Koreans adore their cheese and ramen combination. But don’t let that fool you—still it’s hot!

If you want to know about the next 19 days too, then follow OhMyMisty on TikTok.
