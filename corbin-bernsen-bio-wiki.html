Who is Corbin Bernsen?

Corbin Bernsen is an American actor and director and he is best known for his work on television.

He is also popular for his role as a divorce attorney in the drama series ‘L.A. Law’, as Dr. Alan Feinstone in ‘The Dentist’ and for several other roles.

His father’s name is Harry Bernsen Jr. and his mother’s name is Jeanne Cooper.

His father is a Hollywood movie producer and his mother is a veteran soap actress.

Corbin Bernsen has a brother, Collin Bernsen, and a sister, Caren Bernsen.
