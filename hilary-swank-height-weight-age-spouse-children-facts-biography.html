Hilary Swank is an American actor, voice actor, and film producer who has been cast in a variety of films such as P. S. I Love You, Buffy the Vampire Slayer, Sometimes They Come Back… Again, The Next Karate Kid, Boys Don’t Cry, Birds of America, The Gift, The Resident, Spark, New Year’s Eve, The Affair of the Necklace, Amelia, Million Dollar Baby, Fatale, Cries Unheard: The Donna Yaklich Story, The Black Dahlia, Freedom Writers, The Reaping, You’re Not You, Logan Lucky, 55 Steps, What They Had, I Am Mother, Iron Jawed Angels, Terror in the Family, The Sleepwalker Killing, and The Hunt. In addition to that, her television roles include playing Danielle in the ABC sitcom Camp Wilder, Tiffany Roebuck in the ABC drama series Leaving L.A., Gail Getty in the FX drama series Trust, Emma Green in the Netflix science fiction drama series Away, Eileen in the drama series Alaska Daily, Joey Pogo (voice) in the adult-animated black comedy-drama series BoJack Horseman, and Carly Reynolds in the Fox teen drama series Beverly Hills, 90210.
