Ava Cota- Biography

Ava Cota is a dancer from the United States. An Abby Lee Dance Company alumni who originally appeared on Dance Moms in the third season. She was also chosen for the fourth season of Elite Select Ensemble. In 2016, she competed on So You Think You Can Dance. Tall Girl, a Netflix original film released in 2019, stars her. Ava Cota- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Ava Cota was born in Michigan on April 10, 2002. As of 2021, she is 19 years old. She is of mixed ancestry and has American nationality.As a dancer, she has won multiple national awards and was named Miss Pre Teen Galaxy International in 2014. In 2018, she played “Spin the Wheel” at the Famous Birthdays office. Jeanette Cota, the proprietor of the Broadway Dance Academy facility in Fenton, Michigan, is her mother.
