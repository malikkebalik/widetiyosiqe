Bosch Legacy Season 2 Expected Cast

Titus Welliver (Sons of Anarchy) will reprise his role as Hieronymus “Harry” Bosch in Bosch: Legacy Season 2.

Welliver portrayed the famed detective on Amazon Prime Video for seven seasons before the show ended in June 2021.

In an interview with Entertainment Weekly, he claimed that Bosch: Legacy is not a spin-off. “I fear to call it a spin-off because it isn’t; it’s just the continuous narrative of Harry Bosch,” he explained.

“He’s in a different location, but it’s the same story… A lot is disclosed at the close of Bosch’s final season — I do air quotes on that, ‘final season’ — that lays the groundwork for where these three characters will be when the next show premieres.”

Stephen Chang (Shameless) will reprise his role as Harry’s partner, Maurice “Mo” Bassi, while Mimi Rogers (The Rapture) will reprise her role as Honey “Money” Chandler, a defense attorney who occasionally collaborates with Bosch.

Madison Lintz (The Walking Dead) will reprise her role as Madeline “Maddie” Bosch, Harry’s daughter.

Some guest stars will appear in Bosch: Legacy Season 1, including Gregory Scott Cummins playing Crate and Troy Evans playing Barrel. It’s unclear whether they’ll return for Season 2, but the forthcoming season will almost certainly feature some major guest actors.

Bosch Legacy Season 2 Plot

Because Season 1 is currently showing, it’s difficult to anticipate what will happen in Bosch: Legacy Season 2.

Fans may expect to see Bosch investigate some of Los Angeles’ most violent and intricate crimes in the future.

Bosch: Legacy will also track Bosch’s daughter, Maddie, while she continues her father’s legacy as just an LAPD officer and Bosch’s professional association with Honey Chandler, a high-powered lawyer.

Season 2 of Bosch: Legacy will be based on Michael Connelly’s novel The Crossing, in which Detective Harry Bosch teams up with his half-brother, defense attorney Mickey Haller.

However, it is unknown whether Manuel Garcia-Rulfo will reprise his role as Mickey Haller in Season 2 of Bosch: Legacy. He is presently starring in The Lincoln Lawyer on Netflix.

“Detective Harry Bosch has retired from the LAPD, but his half-brother, defense attorney Mickey Haller, needs his help,” according to the blurb on Connelly’s website.

“The murder charge against his client appears to be unassailable, but Mickey is convinced it’s a ruse. Bosch accepts the case, despite his inclinations to the contrary. He turns the probe inside the police agency with the help of his former LAPD partner Lucia Soto. However, as Bosch approaches the truth, he becomes himself a target.”

Season 1 of Bosch: Legacy airs on Amazon’s Freevee on Fridays.
