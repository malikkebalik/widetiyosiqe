Who is Beth Ostrosky?

Beth Ostrosky is an actress, author, model, and animal rights, activist.

Furthermore, she is the spouse of radio personality Howard Stern.

Beth Ostrosky: Age, Education, Family Background

She was born on 15th July 1972 in Pittsburgh, U.S. She belongs to the American nationality.

Her father’s name is Robert Ostrosky and her mother’s name is Judy Ostrosky.

She has two brothers. Besides this, there is no information regarding her childhood.

Furthermore, Beth Ostrosky has studied at Fox Chapel High School in suburban Fox Chapel, Pennsylvania.

Then she joined the University of Pittsburgh where she was a member of the Delta Delta Delta sorority.

Beth Ostrosky: Professional Career, Awards

Starting her career, she worked as a model while in high school.

In 1996, she has received her first noticeable role as one of the daughters of Ben Stiller’s supposed birth parents,  Flirting with Disaster.

She even acted in a more prominent role four years later, in the film Whipped, with Amanda Peet.

Then Beth Ostrosky appeared on television, appearing in the final season of the G4 show Filter, and the Spike TV series Casino Cinema.

In 2003, 2004, 2005, and 2007, she had placed Stern among FHM’s 100 Sexiest Women of the Year.
