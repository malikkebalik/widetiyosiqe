Before Fame: Education, Early & Family Life

Dove Olivia Cameron, aka Dove Cameron, was born on January 15, 1996, in Seattle, Washington, United States of America.

Her birth name was Chloe Celeste Hosterman, a daughter of Bonnie Wallace and Philip Alan Hosterman.

Her birth parents were separated after a divorce, and she has one older sister, Claire Hosterman.

Dove spent her childhood with her family growing up in Bainbridge Island, Washington, where she went to Sakai Intermediate School for her education.

Cameron and her parents moved to Los Angeles, California, while she was fourteen years old, where Burbank High School replaced her.

Dove was interested in acting from an early age and was also involved in community theater at Bainbridge Performing Arts. 

During her high school in LA, she sang in National Championship Show Choir.
