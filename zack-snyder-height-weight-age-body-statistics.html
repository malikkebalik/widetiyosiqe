Zack Snyder is an American filmmaker who is best known for directing several comic book and superhero films such as 300 (2006), Watchmen (2009), Man of Steel (2013), Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice (2016), and Justice League (Director’s cut) (2021). His other notable directorial ventures include Dawn of the Dead (2004), Legend of the Guardians: The Owls of Ga’Hoole (2010), and Sucker Punch (2011). He has co-written the scripts for some of these films as well as for the superhero film Wonder Woman (2017). He has also produced/executive produced films such as 300: Rise of an Empire (2014), Wonder Woman 1984 (2020), and Army of the Dead (2021). Zack is renowned for the unique visual styling of his films that includes a signature use of dynamic compositions, highly-saturated color schemes, slow-motion shots, brutal fight scenes, and religious symbolism. He had established a production company called Cruel and Unusual Films in 2004 which later came to be known as The Stone Quarry.
