8 Things You Didnt Know About Kimi Katkar

August 2024 · 3 minute read

➡ Kimi Katkar Biography

Kimi Katkar previously worked in the Hindi film industry where she gave brilliant performances in many films. Also, she worked as a model as well and was active throughout the 80s to the early 90s. It is said that she has worked in more than 50 movies including ‘Hum’ which was released in 1991, ‘Adventures of Tarzan’ released in the year 1985, and many more that are worth sharing. The actress has also worked along with Bollywood biggies and got appreciated by everyone for her work and dedication. While she made her acting debut in 1985 through a film named ‘Patthar Dil’ in which she appeared as a supporting star. Since then, she never looked back and continued struggle. Well, to know more about her, let us have a look at these lesser facts!

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Kimi Katkar

1- Real Name

Most people know that her real name is Kimi Katkar as she became famous by her stage name. But today we got to know that her real name is Nayantara Katkar and later, she became famous by her nickname Kimi.

2- Famous For

Kimi Katkar has acted in many films during her career life but she became famous for her song titled ‘Chumma Chumma De De’ in which she performed alongside the veteran actor, Amitabh Bachchan. The song became an instant hit of the year.

3- Hobbies

The actress loves spending time with herself and enjoys cooking, reading books, watching movies, listening to music, and traveling during her leisure time or holidays.

4- Comes from a Family of Artists

Kimi Katkar has a strong background as she comes from a family of artists. Her father was a Junior Artist Supplier for Films and her mother, Tina Katkar was an Actress and Costume Designer.

5- Started Career at 17

In an interview, the actress Kimi Katkar revealed that she took the start of her career at the age of 17. It was the time when she started her career working as a model and accepted multiple modeling assignments.

6- Dated With …

During her career life, it is said that Kimi Katkar dated Sanjay Dutt, Govinda, Mohnish Bahl, and Danny Denzongpa. Well, in 2012, she married to Shantanu Sheorey who is a producer with whom she has a son named Siddharth Sheorey.

7- Didn’t Get Much Fame

The actress made appearances in many films including ‘Mera Lahoo’, ‘Vardi’, ‘Mard Ki Zabaan’, and many others, but unfortunately, she did not get much fame in her acting profession.

8- Second Wife of Shantanu Sheorey

Initially, a film producer, Shantanu Sheorey married Malvika Tiwari who is also an actress. But things did not go well between them, and they parted their ways. Later, he married Kimi Katkar.
