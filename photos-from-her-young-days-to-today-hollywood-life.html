Lisa Bonet is an icon of the 80s and a mother of 3. See her best photos through the years, in honor of her birthday, here.

Lisa Bonet, born on November 16, 1967, was an icon the instant she appear on TV in the 80s, playing Denise Huxtable on The Cosby Show. The series would lead to an Emmy nomination and a spin-off series, A Different World, which ran from 1987 to 1993.

After that Lisa appeared in features like Angel Heart (1987), Enemy of the State (1998), High Fidelity (2000), Biker Boyz (2003), and Road to Paloma (2013). After Cosby, she would return to TV in the 2000s with Life on Mars (2008–2009) and Ray Donovan (2016).

She married Lenny Kravitz in 1987. They share actress daughter Zoe Kravitz, but divorced in 1993. In 2017 Lisa wed Jason Momoa. The couple had two kids, but split in 2022. Want more Lisa Bonet? Check out the gallery for her best photos of all time.
