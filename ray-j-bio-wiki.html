Who is Ray J?

Ray J is a producer and an actor belonging to America. He is popular for his performance on Black Sash (2003), Mars Attacks! (1996), and Aftershock: Earthquake in New York (1999).

Also, he is a television personality, songwriter, and businessman.

Ray J: Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Siblings

This TV personality was born William Ray Norwood Jr. on 17th January 17, 1981, to Willie Norwood and Sonja Norwood in McComb, Mississippi, USA.

He has got an older sister namely Brandy Norwood, a recording artist, and actress. Ray’s ethnicity is All-American.

Furthermore, Ray J initiated performing in television commercials for several companies at the age of just 18.

Ray J: Early Life Profession and Career

In talking about Ray J’s profession and career, an actor firstly initiated his acting career appearing in ‘The Sinbad Show’ as the foster son in 1993. He was then cast in ‘Mars Attacks!’, Tim Burton’s comic science fiction film after the show started in 1994.  In 1996, a comic science fiction film was released. Besides, he appeared in the film titled ‘Steel’ with Shaquille O’Neal, a former basketball player the following year.

Moreover, he appeared on his sister’s show, ‘Moesha’ as a special guest star in 1996. He began appearing regularly in the series after the first three seasons in 1999. Another, he contracted with ‘Elektra Records’ in 1995 and initiated work on his debut album. Both of them wrote and produced 15 songs over the next two years in 1995.

, Ray J and Brandy performed for the song ‘Another Day in Paradise’, a cover of English singer-songwriter Phil Collins in 2001. He then recorded his second studio album, ‘This ain’t a Game’ through ‘Atlantic Records’ on June 19, 2001.

Additionally, Ray started appearing as D-Mack on the final season of UPN sitcom ‘One on One in 2005. He became one of the parts of the dating show, VH1’s ‘For the Love of Ray J’ from 2008 to 2010. He also has worked on Oxygen’s ‘Bad Girls All-Star Battle’ as a host for its entire two-season run.

Ray J recorded his fifth album titled ‘Smoke Cloud TMG & OHB’ on November 30, 2016.
