Killers of the Flower Moon Release Date: Murder Under the Full Moon!

July 2024 · 5 minute read

The director Martin Scorsese is most frequently linked to gangster and wise guy movies from the past. However, the director is branching into a new genre with his upcoming Western Killers of the Flower Moon.

The film, an adaptation of the nonfiction book Killers of the Flower Moon: The Osage Murders and The Birth of the FBI by journalist David Grann, depicts the tale of at least twenty Osage Tribal members who were murdered in Oklahoma in the 1920s.

Grann’s book’s film rights were sold in 2016, a year before it was even released, but the eagerly awaited film’s production didn’t start until 2021. Martin Scorsese was hired after some directors indicated an interest in the undertaking without formally committing.

The COVID-19 pandemic caused shows to be postponed until mid-2021 from their original March 2020 start date. Here is what we currently know about the project.

Killers of the Flower Moon Release Date

The movie’s release date had been set for 2022. Although it was announced in July 2022 that the movie wasn’t ready and will be released in 2023 instead. Killers of the Flower Moon is anticipated to have its premiere in 2023 at a film festival.

Killers of the Flower Moon Cast

Robert de Niro and Leonardo DiCaprio, who have been Scorsese’s longtime inspiration and colleagues in numerous films, were among the recognizable faces he brought on board when he agreed to direct the movie.

Since 1973’s Mean Streets, Scorsese and De Niro have worked together on one short and nine full-length movies, making their partnership one of the most successful and well-known actor-director partnerships ever. Scorsese and de Niro created such beloved movies as Taxi Driver, Raging Bull (for which de Niro received the Academy Award for Best Actor), Goodfellas, Cape Fear, Casino, and The Irishman.

De Niro portrays William Hale, the evil cattle owner who is thought to have planned contract killings of affluent Osage tribe members, in Killers of the Flower Moon. This will be Scorsese and de Niro’s eleventh picture together, and it’s sure to keep up their stellar track record.

Although it barely started in 2002, Scorsese and Leonardo DiCaprio’s partnership is more recent but no less prestigious. After collaborating for the first time on Gangs of New York in 2002, the filmmaker and actor will work together again for Killers of the Flower Moon.

Killers of the Flower Moon Plot

The plot to murder approximately sixty members of the Osage tribe between 1921 and 1925 is detailed in the book Killers of the Flower Moon. The Osage Indian Tribe of Oklahoma received the right to benefit from the vast oil riches that were found in the area toward the end of the 19th century.

The Osage nation was regarded as one of the richest nations in the world due to the tens of millions of dollars that the Osage people generated on their own. The prosperity led to deadly envy among other communities who hadn’t quite struck it as wealthy, and it made the federal government uneasy enough to assign Caucasian guardians to “help” with financial management.
