Who is Martin Starr?

Martin James Pflieger Schienle or simply Martin Starr is an actor and comedian. He is originally from Santa Monica, California. He portrayed Bill Haverchuck in the famous comedy-drama Freaks and Geeks (1999-2000).

Martin Starr also portrayed Roman DeBeers on the comedy series Party Down(2009–2010). He plays the role of Bertram Gilfoyle in the HBO series called Silicon Valley.

The series started in 2014 and continues to broadcast to the present day. He is also known for his roles in movies like Knocked Up (2007), Adventureland (2009), and SpiderMan: Homecoming(2017).

Martin Starr: Early Professional Life, Career

Martin Starr started his career as Bill Haverchuck in the short-lived series called Freaks and Geeks at NBC. After a number of one-time roles and guest appearances on various TV shows, Starr made his way to a mini-series called Revelations in 2005. He also appeared on the CBS sitcom How I Met Your Mother the same year.

In 2007 Martin Starr co-starred in the Hollywood comedy movie called Knocked Up and started gaining success. He then got larger roles in movies like Good Dick and Adventureland. Starr continued to appear on TV shows and series from 2009-2001. He appeared on Starz sitcom Party Down as Roman DeBeers, NTSF:SD:SUV: as Sam Stern and guest appeared on series like Mad Love, Community, and Children’s Hospital.

Martin was chosen to cast the role of Bertram Gilfoyle on the HBO comedy series called Silicon Valley in 2014. The series was nominated for the Emmy Awards for its first two seasons and Martin still holds his role as Bertram Gilfoyle in the fifth season of the series.

Lifetime Achievements and Awards

The HBO comedy series Silicon Valley in which Martin portrays the role of Bertram Gilfoyle was nominated for the Emmy Awards and won the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Single-Camera Picture Editing – Comedy Series (2015).

The series also won the Critics’ Choice Television Award for Best Comedy Series (2014 and 2016), Critics’ Choice Television Award for Best Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series, Satellite Award for Best Television Series – Musical or Comedy, Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Production Design for a Narrative Program (Half-Hour or Less) and many others.
