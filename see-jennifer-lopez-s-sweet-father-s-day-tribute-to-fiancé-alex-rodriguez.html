Multitalented entertainer, Jennifer Lopez shares a touching video which she dedicates to her fiance Alex Rodriguez in celebration of Father's day.

The role of a father in the life of a child cannot be underestimated. While not all fathers are there for their kids, those who are there deserve to be appreciated for the love they shower on their family.

Multitalented entertainer Jennifer Lopez took to Instagram to share a touching video which she dedicated to her fiance Alex Rodriguez in honour of Father's Day celebration.

Jennifer Lopez and Alex Rodriguez are seen arriving to the 2019 CFDA Fashion Awards on June 3, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

The video featured a slideshow of pictures of Lopez, Rodriguez and their blended family. Through the one minute ten seconds clip, a voice-over of Lopez talking about Rodriguez can be heard.

The clips from the video showed the family at different times, having fun and hanging out. Moments from their time on the beach to their time at home were also shown. Captioning the post, Lopez wrote:

"Having you in all of our lives is such a blessing. Watching you with all our babies makes my soul sing. Happy Father's Day macho Bello!!! We love you so so much@arod."

In the video, Rodriguez can be seen playing with his and Lopez's kids. The video paid credence to his goofy side and sweet smiles. Following the post, fans and lover of the couple took to the comments section to share their thoughts and to wish Rodriguez a happy father's day.

The couple has had to put a hold on their plans for now.

Some of the comments stated that Rodriguez was indeed a wonderful person for maintaining a special bond with his kids while also being there for Lopez's kids.

Others stated that he was doing a great job at being a father and fiance to Lopez while some other fans could not help but swoon over how cute the two looked together. Someone said:

"Happy Father's Day Alex!! Wish u all the best in life! you are a [sic] amazing dad to your girls, Max and Emme! So humble and a heart of gold!..."

Last week, the couple decided to surprise Lopez's son Max with a new dog and asked followers to help choose a name for the golden noodle. After much deliberation, they settled on the name Tyson and Max could not be more excited.

Lopez and Rodriguez began dating in March 2017, and in 2018, the couple decided to move in together and blend their families. The two had been previously married and had kids from their former marriages.

Aside from having a blended family, Lopez and Rodriguez ensure that they are hands-on with their kids. They encourage them to help around the house, and recently when they joined the Black Lives Matter movement, the kids made the design for their placards.

Rodriguez proposed in March 2019 and wedding plans were being discussed; however, the couple has had to put a hold on their plans for now as the lockdown has affected their plans. While it might be saddening, Lopez has revealed that she thinks it is for bigger and better things to come their way.
